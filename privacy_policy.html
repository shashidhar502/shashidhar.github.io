2. How the App Works

Insta Youtube Privacy processes shared URLs locally on your device. When you share a link from Instagram or YouTube:

  1. The app receives the shared text/URL through Android's share intent
  2. The URL is processed locally on your device
  3. Tracking parameters (like 'igsh' or 'si') are removed
  4. The cleaned URL is passed back to Android's share menu
  5. No data leaves your device

3. Data Storage
